CHAPTER 1

The Mysterious Benefactor

September, Year One

They tell me I am to write you a letter each month — and I think this is a very queer arrangement, writing letters to someone I have never met who will never write back. But here I am.

I have a room all to myself at Lincoln, with a window that looks over elm trees. I have never had a room to myself before, and the luxury of it is almost beyond bearing.

I've decided to call you Daddy-Long-Legs, because I caught the shadow of your silhouette on the wall as you climbed into your motor car — all long legs and spidery elbows. I hope you don't object.

Yours devotedly,

Jerusha Abbott

(who prefers to be called Judy)

J
October 3rd · Daddy-Long-Legs

Miss Abbott —

Your letters are received. Study hard.
