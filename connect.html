Chapter 1

Jerusha Abbott grows up in a poor family with little emotional support and a constant fear of being left behind in life. When she unexpectedly receives a scholarship from an unknown benefactor to attend college, she eagerly accepts and begins a new chapter of her education. As part of the agreement, she writes monthly emails to her anonymous sponsor, whom she calls “Daddy Long Legs”.

Chapter 2

College life helps Jerusha discover new confidence and independence. Writing becomes a personal outlet, and she begins signing her emails as “Judy,” a version of herself who feels stronger, more courageous, and ready to embrace new opportunities.

Chapter 3

In her sophomore year, Judy begins to feel more at home at BYU-Hawaii. She continues writing her letters.

Chapter 4

Jerusha develops a close relationship with Jervis Pendelton, whom she meets through an online dating app. As both relationships deepen, she notices unusual similarities in the messages she receives and becomes suspicious about their origins.

Chapter 5

Jerusha discovers that both “Daddy Long Legs” and Jervis Pendelton are AI systems created by the institution sponsoring her education. Although shocked to realize that neither relationship involved a real person, she reflects on her personal growth and questions whether the emotions and lessons gained through these artificial connections are any less meaningful.
