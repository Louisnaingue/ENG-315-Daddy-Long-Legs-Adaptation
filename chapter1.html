CHAPTER 1

The Mysterious Benefactor

September, Year One
1
✉

The first Wednesday of every month was a Perfectly Awful Day — cleaning day, bath day, visitors day. But this particular Wednesday changed everything. Jerusha Abbott was called into the matron's office. A trustee, tall and thin, his shadow stretching across the wall like a daddy-long-legs spider, had agreed to be her patron. She would attend Lincoln University. She had only to write him a monthly letter.
